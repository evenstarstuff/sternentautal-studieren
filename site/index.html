Sternentautal Studieren

Deutsch lernen mit Stardew Valley

Vokabeln

  • die Seele noun
    soul
    ...und deine Seele wird von der wachsenden Leere eingenommen. ...and your soul will be consumed by the growing emptiness.
    The very first words of the game. Grandpa comes in swinging.
  • die Leere noun
    emptiness, void
    ...von der wachsenden Leere eingenommen. ...consumed by the growing emptiness.
    A poetic word for the hollow feeling of corporate life. Grandpa knew.
  • die Verbundenheit noun
    connectedness, sense of connection
    Ich habe vergessen, was im Leben wirklich wichtig ist, nämlich Verbundenheit mit anderen Menschen und der Natur. I forgot what is truly important in life, namely connectedness with other people and with nature.
    Grandpa's whole thesis, in one beautiful compound noun.
  • aufgeben verb
    to give up, to abandon
    Also habe ich alles aufgegeben. So I gave everything up.
    A separable verb — the 'auf' splits off to the end. Grandpa made it look easy.
  • gehören verb
    to belong
    ...bin an den Ort gegangen, an den ich wirklich gehöre. ...went to the place where I truly belong.
    One of those quietly heartfelt words. Where do YOU gehören?
  • der Stolz noun
    pride
    Mein ganzer Stolz: Mondschein Hof. My greatest pride: Moonshine Farm.
    Grandpa's entire legacy summed up in two words. No pressure.
  • kostbar adjective
    precious, valuable
    Das war mein kostbarstes Geschenk und jetzt gehört es dir. That was my most precious gift and now it belongs to you.
    From grandpa's letter. Bring tissues.
  • der Kerl noun
    guy, fellow
    ...sag dem alten Kerl 'Hallo' von mir, ja? ...say 'hello' to the old fellow for me, yeah?
    Grandpa's affectionate nickname for Mayor Lewis. Colloquial and very charming.
  • hiesig adjective
    local, of this place
    Ich bin Robin, die hiesige Schreinerin. I am Robin, the local carpenter.
    A fancy way of saying 'local'. Makes you sound like you know the area.
  • aufräumen verb
    to tidy up, to clean up
    Er ist gerade dort und räumt ein wenig für deine Ankunft auf. He is there right now, tidying up a little for your arrival.
    A separable verb. Lewis was apparently doing his best. Results may vary.
  • verwildert adjective
    overgrown, gone wild
    Klar, er ist ein wenig verwildert... Sure, it's a little overgrown...
    Robin's diplomatic opener before describing the disaster that is your farm.
  • das Durcheinander noun
    mess, chaos, disorder
    ...aber unter dem Durcheinander befindet sich guter Boden. ...but under the mess there is good soil.
    Robin finding the silver lining in what sounds like a complete catastrophe.
  • der Fleiß noun
    diligence, hard work
    Mit ein wenig Fleiß hast du hier im Nu Ordnung geschaffen. With a little diligence you'll have things sorted out here in no time.
    Robin's pep talk. She believes in you. The farm does not yet deserve this faith.
  • im Nu phrase
    in no time, in an instant
    ...hast du hier im Nu Ordnung geschaffen. ...you'll have things sorted out here in no time.
    A lovely idiom. 'Nu' is an old word for 'now' — so literally 'in a now'.
  • der Neuankömmling noun
    newcomer, new arrival
    Ah, unser Neuankömmling! Ah, our newcomer!
    Lewis greeting you like you're the guest of honour at a state banquet.
  • das Ereignis noun
    event, occurrence
    Es kommt nicht jeden Tag vor, dass jemand Neues einzieht. Das ist ein ziemlich großes Ereignis! It doesn't happen every day that someone new moves in. That's quite a big event!
    Lewis treating your arrival like a national holiday. Pelican Town must be very quiet.
  • einziehen verb
    to move in
    Du ziehst in die alte Hütte deines Großvaters ein. You're moving into your grandfather's old cottage.
    Another separable verb. The 'ein' travels to the end like a little caboose.
  • rustikal adjective
    rustic
    Es ist ein gutes Haus. Sehr... 'rustikal'. It's a good house. Very... 'rustic'.
    Lewis's air-quoted diplomacy. The pause before 'rustikal' is doing a lot of heavy lifting.
  • schäbig adjective
    shabby, scruffy
    'Schäbig' wäre wohl ein wenig passender. 'Shabby' would probably be a little more fitting.
    Robin's immediate and devastating rebuttal. She is not here for euphemisms.
  • unhöflich adjective
    rude, impolite
    Wie unhöflich! How rude!
    Lewis clutching his pearls after Robin called your house shabby. In front of you. On day one.
  • beeinflussen verb
    to influence, to sway
    Sie will dich nur beeinflussen, damit du ein paar ihrer Verbesserungen für das Haus kaufst. She just wants to influence you so you buy some of her improvements for the house.
    Lewis immediately throwing Robin under the bus. The Pelican Town political drama begins.
  • erkunden verb
    to explore
    Morgen erkundest du dann am besten ein wenig die Stadt. Tomorrow you should explore the town a bit.
    Lewis's parting advice. An extremely useful verb for adventurers.
  • schätzen verb
    to appreciate, to value
    Das wissen sie sicher zu schätzen. They will surely appreciate that.
    Lewis being optimistic about the townspeople. Also means 'to estimate' — a wonderfully versatile verb.
  • der Enkel noun
    grandchild, grandson
    ...und für meinen ganz besonderen Enkel: ...and for my very special grandchild:
    Grandpa's words before the letter begins. 'Ganz besonders' = very special. You are loved.
  • versiegelt adjective
    sealed
    Ich möchte, dass du diesen versiegelten Umschlag bekommst. I want you to receive this sealed envelope.
    The mysterious envelope! What's inside?! (Don't open it yet. Hab Geduld.)
  • die Geduld noun
    patience
    Nein, nein, öffne ihn noch nicht... hab Geduld. No, no, don't open it yet... be patient.
    Grandpa teasing you from beyond the grave. Iconic.
  • die Last noun
    burden, load, weight
    Der Tag wird kommen, an dem du dich von der Last des modernen Lebens erdrückt fühlen wirst... The day will come when you will feel crushed by the burden of modern life...
    Grandpa's prophecy. He was right. That's why you're here.
  • die Veränderung noun
    change, transformation
    ...dann wirst du eine Veränderung bitter nötig haben. ...you will desperately need a change.
    The entire premise of Stardew Valley in five words.
  • tonnenweise adverb
    tons of, by the ton
    Gute Futter- und Fischfangmöglichkeiten und tonnenweise Freiflächen. Good foraging and fishing opportunities and tons of open space.
    Literally 'in the manner of tonnes'. German compound adverbs are a gift to the world.
  • der Fischräucherer noun
    fish smoker
    Du beginnst mit einem Fischräucherer. You start with a fish smoker.
    The Flussbett farm's unique starting bonus. A compound noun so specific it's almost art: Fisch + Räucherer (one who smokes things).
  • Added 17 July 2026:
  • die Pastinake noun
    parsnip
    Du hast 15 Samen für Pastinaken erhalten! You received 15 parsnip seeds!
    The parsnip that started it all. Lewis, you legend.
  • die Kleinigkeit noun
    small thing, little something
    Hier eine Kleinigkeit, um dir den Start zu erleichtern. Here's a little something to make your start easier.
    Lewis calls 15 seeds 'a little something'. Very Lewis.
  • der Wahrsager noun
    fortune teller
    Wähle Sendung: Wahrsager Choose programme: Fortune Teller
    One of four TV channels. He can hear the ghosts. Concerning.
  • der Wetterbericht noun
    weather report
    Wähle Sendung: Wetterbericht Choose programme: Weather Report
    Wetter (weather) + Bericht (report). German compound nouns are just two words holding hands.
  • die Unterhaltung noun
    entertainment, conversation
    ...deiner Lieblingsquelle für Wetter, Neuigkeiten und Unterhaltung. ...your favourite source for weather, news and entertainment.
    One word meaning both 'entertainment' AND 'conversation'. Multitasking.
  • sonnig adjective
    sunny
    Morgen wird es den ganzen Tag klar und sonnig sein. Tomorrow it will be clear and sunny all day.
    Your very first weather forecast. A good omen for Day 1 on the farm.
  • der Geist noun
    ghost, spirit
    Ah... ja, ich kann die Geister flüstern hören... Ah... yes, I can hear the ghosts whispering...
    The fortune teller opens with this. Casual ghost communication. Nothing to worry about.
  • flüstern verb
    to whisper
    ...ich kann die Geister flüstern hören... ...I can hear the ghosts whispering...
    A beautiful verb. The ghosts are doing it. Perhaps about you.
  • genervt adjective
    annoyed, irritated
    Die Geister sind heute etwas genervt. Das Glück wird nicht auf deiner Seite sein. The ghosts are a bit annoyed today. Luck will not be on your side.
    The ghosts are annoyed. On your first day. Already. Great start.
  • das Glück noun
    luck, happiness
    Das Glück wird nicht auf deiner Seite sein. Luck will not be on your side.
    One word for both luck AND happiness. The ghosts are taking yours today.
  • der Grünschnabel noun
    greenhorn, newbie
    Diese Sendung heute ist euch Grünschnäbeln gewidmet! Today's show is dedicated to you greenhorns!
    Literally 'green beak'. The farming show's affectionate term for beginners. That's you!
  • die Ernte noun
    harvest
    ...während ihr auf eure erste Ernte wartet! ...while you wait for your first harvest!
    The whole point of Stardew Valley, in one word.
  • die Zutaten noun
    ingredients
    Zutaten: 2x Holz Ingredients: 2x Wood
    Essential crafting menu word. Also useful if you ever cook something.
  • im Griff haben phrase
    to have under control, to keep in check
    Hält Tiere und Gras im Griff! Keeps animals and grass in check!
    The wooden fence's job description. Literally 'in the grip'. A very satisfying idiom.
  • die Ohren spitzen phrase
    to prick up one's ears, to pay attention
    Spitzt die Ohren: Prick up your ears:
    Literally 'sharpen your ears'. The farming show's way of saying 'listen up'. Immediately charming.
